Intresting Fact about Camels

  • Camel is a domestic animal in desert.Its average life is 45-60 years.The suitable weather for camel is hot and dry.
  • Camel can walk long time in the desert.Camels are used in a long journey and many works in the desert.

Intresting Fact about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • These colorful lizards are known as one of the few animals that can change skin color.

Intresting Fact about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are intelligent birds.They can identify the same person on 2 different images.
  • Pigeons can easily detect distant storms, earthquakes and volcanic eruptions since they have a very sharp sense of hearing

Intresting Fact about Teddy

  • The word 'Teddy' represents the famous soft toy 'Teddy Bear'.

Intresting Fact about Panda

  • The giant panda is native to China.It has a black and white coat that features large black patches around its eyes.
  • A giant panda cub weighs only around 150 grams (5 oz) at birth.Adult males can weigh up to 150 kg (330 lb).

Intresting Fact about Cicada

  • The cicadas are a superfamily, the Cicadoidea, of insects in the order Hemiptera
  • They are in the suborder Auchenorrhyncha, along with smaller jumping bugs
